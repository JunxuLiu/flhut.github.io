About Me

I am a Ph.D student at Renmin Uinversity of China. My research interest is privacy-preserving machine learning, specialized in federated learning and differential privacy. I used to study as a visiting scholar at Emory University hosted by Prof. Li Xiong in 2020. I made a lot of friends there. All of them are very friendly, interesting and smart. They, espically Prof. Xiong, Jinfei and Jian, have taught me a lot of things, what strikes me most is that I need to be more patient, more motivated, and more hard working to become a well-qualified researcher. I really appreciate it.

I enjoy physical activities in my free time, which, I believe, are the best way to combat anxiety. I am good, I think, at badminton, not bad at table tennis, and a tennis beginner. I am also a fan of Japanese anime, NARUTO, Shingeki no Kyojin and Kimetsu no Yaiba are my favorite up to now.
